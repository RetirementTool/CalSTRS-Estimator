Member-Only Benefit — Beneficiary Options Not Calculated

Additional planning required

This calculator shows your Member-Only Benefit — the highest monthly amount available at retirement, paid for your lifetime only. The benefit stops when you die. If you want to provide a continuing monthly income to a spouse or beneficiary after your death, you must elect a Beneficiary Option at retirement, which permanently reduces your monthly check.

100% Option

Your beneficiary receives your full monthly amount after you die. Results in the largest reduction to your monthly benefit.

75% Option

Your beneficiary receives 75% of your monthly amount. A moderate reduction to your benefit.

50% Option

Your beneficiary receives half your monthly amount. Results in the smallest reduction to your benefit.

The reduction is calculated using an option factor based on your age, your beneficiary's age, and the option you elect. If your beneficiary predeceases you, your benefit rises back to the full Member-Only amount. This election is irrevocable 30 days after your first payment is issued. → Learn more about beneficiary options at CalSTRS.com · Official CalSTRS Retirement Benefit Calculator
